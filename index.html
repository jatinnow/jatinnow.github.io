Vipassana Meditation Retreat

Dhamma Nilaya, Jaipur

Sep 2021 • 10-day Silent Retreat

Vipassana is a 2,500-year-old meditation technique rediscovered by the Buddha that teaches you to observe—and not react to—pain and pleasure as impermanent sensations. I completed a 10-day silent retreat at Dhamma Nilaya in Jaipur, rising at 4 AM to meditate until 9 PM with only brief breaks, no devices, talking, or distractions.

The experience was intense—physically and mentally painful at times—but transformative: by day 2 I could focus on my breath for over an hour, and by persevering through sleepless nights on day 6, I learned resilience. Feel free to ask me anything about the practice!
